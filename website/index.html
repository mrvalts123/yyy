How can I use Limit Orders & DCA?

You can use limit orders to specify the price and size you wish to buy JUP at, or use DCA to swap into JUP over a period of time. Demand will be very high at the start and you might not get your orders filled if you select a very competitive price. See the next question for some tips.

How do I increase the odds of my transaction succeeding?

In times of network congestion, such as airdrop claims, you can try increasing your priority fees. You can change your settings on the bottom footer. You might also need to loosen your price targets and slippage tolerance to get your trades through. Bear in mind that your slippage setting and max/min price settings are there to protect you, so it has to be within your tolerance.

How do I use MockJUP and MockUSDC?

MockJUP is used to simulate the real $JUP token during JUP launch. You can claim MockJUP just like your airdrop, or buy it when trading begins with the MockUSDC that you get from the faucet. MockJUP can be traded on this page, using Swap, DCA or Limit Orders. You can also provide liquidity for it on AMMs such as Meteora.
